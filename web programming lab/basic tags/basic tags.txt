<p><b>Adi Shankara Institute of Engineering and Technology</b>is one of the prestigious technological institutes in Kerala offering <i><b>NBA accredited</b></i> programs,<i>(CSE, ECE, ME, EE)</i>
and having industry collaborations with the top Techno Giants and international collaborations with various universities abroad.</p>
<p>deally located in an idyllic ambiance, Adi Shankara Institute of Engineering & Technology (ASIET) kindles vibrant memories of the serene presence of Jagadguru Adi Shankara. <br>
Affiliated to A P J Abdul Kalam Technological University, accredited by NBA and approved by AICTE, ASIET is the first self-financing technical education center to be awarded the ISO 9001: 2008 certification.<br>
Located just 5 km from Kochi international airport, ASIET has a dynamic academic atmosphere in an idyllic environment. 
Fifteen batches of B.Tech students have passed out from the college to date and they occupy responsible positions in prestigious organizations in India and abroad.<br>
Established in 2001 and adeptly run by the Sringeri Mutt with the benign blessings of Sree Sree Bharathitheertha Mahaswamigal, the College believes in keeping a proactive approach for the overall development of the students.</p>
